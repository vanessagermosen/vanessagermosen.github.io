About Me

I'm a Design Director living in New York City. I became interested in Front-End Web Design and Development because my team is currently being tasked with designing artwork for various digital projects (apps, web games, etc.). I want to make sure I understand the process and limitations of web design so that I can better manage the visual assets we provide.

My Mission

As I learn more about front-end, I hope that I can become as well rounded as possible in my current position. I like to work as an artist with a steady income and my current job allows me to do just that with the added bonus of a team of people I respect and enjoy working with. Ideally I would like to have a thorough understanding of web design, technical terms, and web limitations so that when projects are assigned to me I can communicate effectively between what our producers and coders request and what my team and myself can create.
